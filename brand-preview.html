Typeface directions

Honest context: our current display serif, Fraunces, has become the signature font of tasteful AI-built wellness apps — including the other Hearth. These five pairings are rendered live in the F6 palette (heading font + body font each); the eventual swap is a three-line code change. Judge each by: does the headline feel like a hearth? does the body read effortlessly at phone sizes? Recommendation: T1 Young Serif + Figtree — hand-set storybook warmth nobody else in the space is wearing.

T1 · Young Serif + Figtree — the recommendation
