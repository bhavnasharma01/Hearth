Notes for the review

What F4's own exploration card said: "The most understated finalist: galleries-and-linen calm where the ember flame is the only saturated thing on screen. Maximum sophistication, minimum noise. Risk: greys can read cool in poor light; the warmth must come from type and photography."
What stays the same in both palettes: the layouts, Zilla Slab + Source Sans 3, and the heart-flame mark (which recolours itself to each palette's flame tokens, exactly as a real reskin would). The app is built so switching is one block in one file, so if Stone & Ember wins the room, adopting it is small work; the hand-updated pieces are the favicon, the logo files, and the email hexes.
A fair test: look at both on your phone, outdoors and in dim light, before deciding. Toggle, don't recall. The screens are identical twins; only the clothes change.
