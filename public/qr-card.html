Printable QR cards

Two formats for events: a 4×6 postcard to stand on a table, and a sheet of eight wallet-size cards to hand out. The QR points to myhearthapp.ca/practitioners and has been scan-verified. The dashed lines are cut guides.

To print: File → Print (or Ctrl/Cmd+P), paper size Letter, scale 100% (not "fit to page"). Page 1 is the postcard, page 2 the hand-out cards. Card stock if you have it.
