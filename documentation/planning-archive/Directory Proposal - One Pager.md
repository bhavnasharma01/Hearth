# Hearth — Proposal

*One simple, free place to find local practitioners and see what's happening in our community.*

*Hearth* — the warm center of a home that people have always gathered around. A fitting name for a space whose whole purpose is to draw us out of our separate homes and into shared warmth, healing, and connection.

## The idea

Our community already holds two beautiful things: a wealth of gifted practitioners — massage and somatic therapists, energy healers, facilitators, medicine people — and a calendar full of conscious events. Right now they live in separate, hard-to-navigate places, and the calendar in particular is clunky to read on a phone.

This is a proposal for one warm, phone-friendly website that brings both together. In a single place, anyone can **find a practitioner** *and* **see what's coming up** — ecstatic dance, breathwork, ceremonies, sessions — laid out beautifully and far easier to scan than Google Calendar.

It would be **completely free to build and run**, require **almost no ongoing admin**, and reuse the tools we already have.

## How it works (in plain terms)

1. **Practitioners add themselves** through a simple online form — the same easy experience as adding a calendar event. No app, no login, no account.
2. **Event hosts change nothing.** Events keep flowing through the existing events form and Google Calendar, curated just as they are today.
3. **One beautiful website pulls both together** — a searchable practitioner directory, and a clean, mobile-friendly list of upcoming events read live from our calendar. Anyone with the link can open it on their phone, search, filter, and tap to contact a practitioner or see an event.
4. **The community keeps it healthy.** Anyone can flag a listing that looks off. Flags don't do anything automatically — they simply send a quiet heads-up to you or Curtis to take a look. That keeps any one person from unfairly targeting a business, and keeps the workload to an occasional glance.

The whole thing is shared through WhatsApp as a single link — the same way you already point people to the calendar.

## Two options

**Option A — Hub + a dedicated WhatsApp group**
We build the website above *and* add a new WhatsApp group focused on practitioners. The website is the lasting, searchable home; the group keeps the warm, human side alive — people can still ask "does anyone know a good osteopath?" and get personal recommendations. The group's pinned message holds the website link and the "add yourself" form.

**Option B — Hub only (WhatsApp group optional)**
We build just the website and link to it from the existing channels. Whether to add a dedicated practitioner WhatsApp group is left entirely to you — you can always add one later, with no extra work on the site itself.

*Both options use the exact same website underneath. The only difference is whether we create a new WhatsApp group now or leave that to you.*

## What it costs and what it asks of you

- **Money:** nothing. Everything runs on free tools (Google Forms, Google Sheets, the existing Google Calendar, free web hosting).
- **Your time:** near zero. Listings post on their own, events already flow through the calendar, and you or Curtis are only pinged in the rare case a listing gets flagged. This is actually *less* hands-on than the calendar runs today.
- **From me:** I'll build it, set it up, and hand you something simple the community can run without needing a tech person day to day.
